Detailed Description

A simple magnetic diffusion test in cylindrical coordinates.

PURPOSE: A simple magnetic diffusion test in cylindrical coordinates withno pressure or tension forces.

AUTHOR: Chang-Goo Kim

IMPORTANT NOTICE: To perform this test, the main integrator MUST be turned off! iprob = 1; Eigenmode test; IC Bz(r,phi,0)=J_m(lambda_m,n*R)*cos(m*phi); BC Bz(0,phi,t)<infty, Bz(1,phi,t)=0 for R and periodic for phi; Solution: Bz(r,phi,t)=Bz(r,phi,0)*exp(-eta_Ohm/lambda_m,n^2*t) iprob = 2; 1D Cylindrical test with IC Bz(x,0)=1-x^2
