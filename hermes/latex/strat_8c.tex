\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Problem generator for stratified 3D shearing sheet. 

P\+U\+R\+P\+O\+SE\+: Problem generator for stratified 3D shearing sheet. Based on the initial conditions described in \char`\"{}\+Three-\/dimensional Magnetohydrodynamic
  Simulations of Vertically Stratified Accretion Disks\char`\"{} by Stone, Hawley, Gammie \& Balbus.

Several different field configurations and perturbations are possible\+: ifield = 1 -\/ Bz=B0 sin(x1) field with zero-\/net-\/flux \mbox{[}default\mbox{]} ifield = 2 -\/ uniform Bz ifield = 3 -\/ B=(0,B0cos(kx$\ast$x1),B0sin(kx$\ast$x1))= zero-\/net flux w helicity ifield = 4 -\/ uniform By, but only for $\vert$z$\vert$$<$2 ifield = 5 -\/ By with constant \textbackslash{}beta versus z ifield = 6 -\/ flux tube at disk center from Hirose et al. ifield = 7 -\/ zero field everywhere


\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item ipert = 1 -\/ random perturbations to P and V \mbox{[}default, used by H\+GB\mbox{]}
\end{DoxyItemize}

Code must be configured using --enable-\/shearing-\/box

R\+E\+F\+E\+R\+E\+N\+CE\+: Stone, J., Hawley, J. \& Balbus, S. A., ApJ 463, 656-\/673 (1996) Hawley, J. F. \& Balbus, S. A., ApJ 400, 595-\/609 (1992) ~\newline
