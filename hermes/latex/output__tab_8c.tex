\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Functions for writing output in tabular format. 

P\+U\+R\+P\+O\+SE\+: Functions for writing output in tabular format. With S\+MR, dumps are made for all levels and domains, unless nlevel and ndomain are specified in $<$output$>$ blocks.
