\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Problem generator for particle feedback test in 2D. 

P\+U\+R\+P\+O\+SE\+: Problem generator for particle feedback test in 2D. The particles and gas are initialized to have the same total momentum in the opposite direction. Both have uniform density. One test particle is picked up for precision test.
