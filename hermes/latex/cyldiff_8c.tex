\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
A simple magnetic diffusion test in cylindrical coordinates. 

P\+U\+R\+P\+O\+SE\+: A simple magnetic diffusion test in cylindrical coordinates withno pressure or tension forces.

A\+U\+T\+H\+OR\+: Chang-\/\+Goo Kim

I\+M\+P\+O\+R\+T\+A\+NT N\+O\+T\+I\+CE\+: To perform this test, the main integrator M\+U\+ST be turned off! iprob = 1; Eigenmode test; IC Bz(r,phi,0)=J\+\_\+m(lambda\+\_\+m,n$\ast$\+R)$\ast$cos(m$\ast$phi); BC Bz(0,phi,t)$<$infty, Bz(1,phi,t)=0 for R and periodic for phi; Solution\+: Bz(r,phi,t)=Bz(r,phi,0)$\ast$exp(-\/eta\+\_\+\+Ohm/lambda\+\_\+m,n$^\wedge$2$\ast$t) iprob = 2; 1D Cylindrical test with IC Bz(x,0)=1-\/x$^\wedge$2
